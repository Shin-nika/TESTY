Waffles

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • Vegetable oil (for greasing the pan)
  • Toppings of your choice (maple syrup, fresh fruit, whipped cream, etc.)

Preparation Method

In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In another bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter until smooth.
Pour the liquid mixture into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Gently stir with a spoon or whisk until just combined. Do not overmix, as this can make the pancakes heavy. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Lightly grease the skillet with vegetable oil.
Use about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake. Pour the batter into the hot skillet and spread it slightly to form a round pancake. Cook until small bubbles appear on the surface of the pancake and the edges are lightly golden brown. This takes about 2-3 minutes. Flip the pancake with a spatula and cook for another 1-2 minutes, or until completely golden brown.
Transfer the pancake to a plate and repeat the process with the remaining batter.

Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, fresh fruit, whipped cream, etc.

Additional Information

This recipe makes approximately 8 medium pancakes. You can adjust the size of the pancakes to your preference. Enjoy your delicious American pancakes!

Hot chocolate

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Grated chocolate (optional)

Preparation Method

In a saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until it begins to boil. Stir occasionally to prevent the milk from sticking to the bottom of the pan. Add the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt to the hot milk. Stir well until the chocolate and sugar are completely dissolved. Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the hot chocolate cook for another 2 to 3 minutes, stirring constantly. This will help thicken the drink slightly. Remove the pan from the heat and add the vanilla extract. Mix well. Pour the hot chocolate into cups or mugs. If desired, add whipped cream on top to decorate and sprinkle with grated chocolate. Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious hot chocolate!

Additional Information

Remember that you can adjust the amount of sugar according to your personal taste. Additionally, you can also add other ingredients, such as cinnamon powder or marshmallows, to give your hot chocolate an extra touch.
